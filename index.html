Flex | Branding + Merchandise
Pearl Mendonza
Posted on 21 Nov, 2019
Like
Liked
Flex is a learning initiative by Fynd to help individuals extend their skills with learning opportunities and practical training to match their goals and objectives. They conduct learning and development events that help the people at Fynd to be the best version of themselves and not just grow but be extraordinary.
We created a simple yet striking, bold yet elegant brand identity, brand language and collaterals for Flex that would not only appeal to the people at Fynd but also reflect what Flex is all about. Played with simple, clean shapes and straightforward dynamics and compositions

GlamAR Illustrations
Pearl Mendonza
Posted on 03 Dec, 2019
Like
Liked
GlamAR is a machine learning & makeup expertise, built to create self-assisted tryout experience for customers. It basically allows customers to quickly try out makeup solutions in Augmented reality environment. We created the brand identity for GlamAR and also a hero illustration for the website and app, which depicted a makeup room environment with products and styles augmented through a mobile screen.
Core Values -Divided by Teams, United by Values
Pearl Mendonza
Posted on 08 Jan, 2020
Like
Liked
The core values are paramount to everything people do at Fynd. They reflect that Fynd is a place unlike any other. It’s a place where you can solve complex challenges for the customers or the company, alongside thoughtful individuals who lead with curiosity, empathy, and optimism. You work hard, but you also craft moments that allows the community to pause, create, and connect. As a community of managers, engineers, designers, and more, these shared values and beliefs influence the work that you do, and the impact you hope to achieve. Not just that, it helps to further solidify your identity as a community.

Meet our Team
Fynd is a lean tech startup brewing in Mumbai, India. We call ourselves a design led company because designers play an important role at Fynd. We conceptualise new product ideas and also bring innovations and improvements in our existing products and services. With experience in art, graphics and motion graphics, we also provide design solutions for digital, print, branding, packaging, illustrations and animation.
