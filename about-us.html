Fynd is a lean tech startup brewing in Mumbai, India. We call ourselves a design led company because designers play an important role at Fynd. We conceptualise new product ideas and also bring innovations and improvements in our existing products and services. With experience in art, graphics and motion graphics, we also provide design solutions for digital, print, branding, packaging, illustrations and animation.
